[mlcourse.ai](https://mlcourse.ai) is an open Machine Learning course by [OpenDataScience](https://ods.ai/en/). The course is designed to perfectly balance theory and practice. You can take part in several Kaggle Inclass competitions held during the course. The next (and final) session launches on **September 2, 2019.** Fill in [this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/10HAN5huM996snUKjsNYyT_oOlm2uOsTKulKurb3oiNM/) to participate.
